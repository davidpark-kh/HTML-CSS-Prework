Trip to Europe

A short blog post about our trip throughout northern Europe after we moved to Scotland in 2017.

The first city with which we kicked off our Europe trip was Copenhagen in Denmark. Aesthetically, Copenhagen and Amsterdam were probably my two favorite cities. Vibe-wise, I probably liked Paris the most. People would start eating dinner around 9pm and take their time with their meals, often sitting at the restaurant and drinking wine and smoking cigarettes until midnight. Completely my type of life.
